# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 3, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to MacroLog ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our nutrition tracking application available at macrolog.online (the "Service").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, and password (encrypted)
- **Health Information**: Age, gender, weight, height, activity level, and nutrition goals
- **API Credentials**: Your OpenAI API key (encrypted and stored securely)

### Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

- **Meal Entries**: Food descriptions, meal times, and nutritional data
- **Activity Logs**: Login times, features used, and interaction patterns
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers
- **Performance Data**: Service performance metrics and error logs

### Data from Third-Party Services

- **OpenAI API**: We send your meal descriptions to OpenAI's API for nutritional analysis. This data is processed according to OpenAI's privacy policy.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Service Delivery**: To provide, maintain, and improve our nutrition tracking services
2. **Account Management**: To create and manage your account
3. **AI Processing**: To send meal descriptions to OpenAI for nutritional analysis using your API key
4. **Communication**: To send service-related notifications, updates, and support responses
5. **Analytics**: To understand usage patterns and improve user experience
6. **Security**: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
7. **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption**: All sensitive data, including API keys and passwords, are encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- **Secure Transmission**: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS protocols
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls limit employee access to personal data
- **Regular Audits**: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

### Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Meal Entries**: Retained for the duration of your account or until you delete them
- **API Keys**: Encrypted and stored securely; deleted when you remove them or close your account
- **Logs**: Service logs are retained for 90 days for security and performance monitoring

## Your OpenAI API Key

### How We Handle Your API Key

- Your OpenAI API key is **encrypted** before storage in our database
- We use your API key **only** to make requests to OpenAI on your behalf for nutritional analysis
- We **never** share your API key with third parties
- You maintain full control and ownership of your API key
- You can update or remove your API key at any time

### API Usage

- All API calls to OpenAI are made from our secure servers
- We do not store the full content of API responses beyond the nutritional data extracted
- Usage costs are billed directly to your OpenAI account

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

### Service Providers

- **OpenAI**: Meal descriptions are sent to OpenAI's API for nutritional analysis
- **Hosting Providers**: We use secure hosting services to store and process data
- **Analytics Services**: Anonymized usage data may be shared with analytics providers

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, including:

- Court orders or subpoenas
- Legal processes or government investigations
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety
- Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

### Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice on our Service of any change in ownership.

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Export**: Download your meal history and nutritional data
- **Opt-Out**: Unsubscribe from promotional communications

### How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at **care@macrolog.online**. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

### Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from your profile settings. Upon deletion:

- All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
- Meal entries and nutritional data will be removed
- API keys will be securely destroyed
- Some information may be retained for legal compliance purposes
